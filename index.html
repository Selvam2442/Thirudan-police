How to Play

  1. 1

    Enter names and select rounds. Roles are assigned secretly.

  2. 2

    Pass the phone to the person named on screen. Hide the screen from others to view your secret role!

  3. 3

    Once everyone has seen their roles, the Police must look at everyone's real-life facial expressions and guess who the Thirudan (Thief) is.

  4. 4

    Right guess = 500 pts for Police. Wrong guess = 500 pts for Thirudan!
